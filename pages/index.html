Who we are

We are a virtual graphic design studio created by a squad of extraordinary designers. Born from the hell of the pandemic, we united (virtually) making this marvelous team. Collaborating remotely, we are inspired by the brutalism style of design because there is always beauty even in midst of chaos.

Mainly focusing on creating various kinds of streetwear clothing with more than 1000+ designs already made. We also create designs for various needs: branding, social media, promotional, etc.

Basically, everything you need, we got it!

3

Kemsgraphics was originally used as a personal branding of one of the studio members. But as development progressed, as the number of requests from clients continued to arrive, it was finally realized that it was time to develop till now there are 9 of us.

The value of brutalism start when we’re too tired being chained under coorporate strict rules as part of agency. There we tried to look for a way out, to break the boundaries. and there we discover brutalist, where we can express our true self, without being chained by specific rules, and explore as far as we want. Apparently so many people fell in love with it. In time spans of just 1 year, we already made more than 1000 designs, all based on our clients order.

There we realize, its not just. But, there are many people out there who need help to be themself, to express their self. Hopefully, we can help you too to express yourself to the fullest.

Kemsgraphics was isnt just your average order-taking studio. We are proud to be go-to partner for you! Feel free to let us know your problems and let us help you solve them! Designs is more than just our work, but its our passions, its our life. We can also help you build your ideas, based on our experiences and vast portfolios. By looking into the right questions and challenging the creative vision we ensure to provide our the best possible of our effort for the best result for our clients.
